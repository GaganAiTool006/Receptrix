FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about getting Receptrix answering for your business.

What is Receptrix?+
Receptrix is an AI receptionist app for healthcare, educational institutions, salons, restaurants, and hotels. It automates inbound appointment booking and outbound calls, helping businesses manage customer communication efficiently — all without missing a call or a booking.
Do I need a new phone number?+
No. Your business keeps using its existing customer care number. Inbound calls are simply forwarded to Receptrix, so customers never notice a change. Outbound calls go out from a separate, dedicated Receptrix number.
How does pricing and the credit system work?+
₹1,000 per month includes 1,000 credits. Inbound appointment booking costs 52 credits per appointment, and outbound calling costs 6 credits per minute. You can top up anytime from ₹500 to ₹5,000, and credits never expire.
What can outbound calls be used for?+
Outbound calls are great for payment reminders, appointment follow-ups, promotional messages, and general notifications. Just enter the recipient's number and your message, and Receptrix AI places the call and delivers it automatically.
Which industries is Receptrix built for?+
Receptrix is designed for healthcare providers, educational institutions, salons, restaurants, and hotels — any business where missed calls mean missed appointments, enquiries, or revenue.
